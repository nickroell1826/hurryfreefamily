Questions

Everything you want to know.

What age kids are these guides for? +
The guides are designed for families with elementary and middle school kids. The questions and activities are written to be accessible at those ages while still being genuinely interesting for adults. Families with younger or older kids have used them successfully too — the conversations go wherever your family takes them.
How much time does each week take? +
The dinner conversation itself takes about 15–20 minutes. The daily habit is designed to take under 5 minutes. The family challenge is usually 30–60 minutes and happens once during the week at a time that works for you. You can do as much or as little as fits your family's season.
What format do I get? How do I access the guides? +
The guides are delivered as HTML files — they work beautifully on any phone, tablet, or computer. You can pull them up at the dinner table on your phone, or print them out if you prefer paper. They're designed to be readable in low light and easy to scroll through mid-conversation.
Do I need to have read the books these are based on? +
Not at all. The guides stand completely on their own. Some of them were inspired by books (like Ruthless Elimination of Hurry and The Anxious Generation) but all the content is original — you don't need to have read anything to use them. We do recommend the books for parents who want to go deeper, but it's never required.
Can I use these at church or in a small group? +
The individual guides are licensed for personal and family use. If you're interested in using them with a church congregation, small group, or ministry, we offer church licensing — reach out to us directly to discuss. We'd love to get these into more families through your church.
What if my family misses a week? +
Pick it back up whenever you can. These guides are designed to be forgiving — missing a week doesn't break the experience. Some families do one guide over a longer stretch than intended. What matters is that you come back to the table. The guide will be there when you do.
